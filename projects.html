University Database

In this project, I worked alongside two friends to create a small database that holds professor records, student records, and a variety of other entities with their respective attributes. To lay out the framework for this project, we began by designing both an ER Diagram and Relational Model as our schemas in order to help us structure our database entitities and their relations. Once this was complete, we utilized MySQL for our database tables, and populated them with sample data. Next, I worked on the front-end design of the website, utilizing HTML5 and CSS3 to create pages and forms to test user queries. Four queries are accessible, and I subsequently used PHP to link the user's input with our database, in which their requests would be processed and the desired results would be dispalyed.

Matrix Calculator

Within this project, I worked with a long-time friend of mine to create a fully functional Matrix Calculator. This calculator is capable of performing basic matrix operations such as matrix addition, matrix subtraction, and scalar multiplication. However, it is also capable of more complex operations such as matrix inversion and matrix multiplication. To turn this idea of ours into a reality, we utilized various technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and the ReactJS library, with the entirety of the project being developed in VSCode.
